Support Measures for Counting Frequent Patterns in Single-Graph Databases

2017–2020

The work addresses support measure design and implementation for single-graph frequent pattern mining. It maps support computation into optimization problems for high-performance polynomial and non-polynomial calculations with strong counting quality.
